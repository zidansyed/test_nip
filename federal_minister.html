Federal Minister

Mr. Mian Riaz Hussain Pirzada (IPC)

Inter Provincial Coordination

Mian Prizada was born on August, 1, 1948 in Bahawalpur, Punjab. He has been elected as MNA for this third time on an NA seat. He is politically affiliated with PML. An Agriculturist and advocate by profession, Mian Riaz Hussain Pirzada received his LL.B Degree from University Law College, Lahore in 1969. He was previously elected as MNA for the terms f 1993-1997 and 2008-2013 and held the portfolios of Minister for Health in 2011 and Minister for Professional and Technical Training in 2011-12. He has also served as MPA Punjab Assembly for the term of 1985-1988 and held the portfolio of Provincial Minister in 1988. Married, he has a son and six daughters. He has visited countries such as USA, UK, Iran, Kuwait and Turkey. He enjoys riding and golf.
